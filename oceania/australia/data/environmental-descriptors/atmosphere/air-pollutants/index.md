## Air Pollutants

![Air Pollutants](/assets/img/wales/big/pollutants.jpg)

Air pollutants include gasses such as nitrous oxide, ammonia, sulfur dioxide and particulate matter. Their concentrations inform about air quality that influences plant and animal health.
